Frequently Asked Questions


Do you offer sitting for cats or other animals?

Absolutely! A majority of our hosts welcome cats, birds, and an assortment of other pets. Click on the contact information provided in your search and request a quote for the particular pet service you need.

What if my dog has specific needs?

Whether your pet is elderly, has health needs, needs to be ran alot, etc. we have thousands of experienced professionals willing to watch your pup! We recommend browsing through sitters in your area and reaching out to them explaining the situation prior to booking, just to make sure they're the perfect fit for your pet.

What should i bring when i drop my dog off?

It is very important to bring enough food that will last the duration of the trip. You should also make sure to bring a leash, collar, food/water bowls and any medicine or special instructions that your host might need. You may also want to bring a bed and a blanket, we recommend discussing this with the host prior to the trip so ensure that you and your pets vacation goes as smoothly as possible!

How does PetCation work?

We know how difficult it is to find someone you trust to look after your fur child, so we created a platform to glance at services side by side and find the right people for you! Simply enter your zip code or city and the nearest pet sitters and kennels will display accordingly, along with their contact number, location, and reviews.
